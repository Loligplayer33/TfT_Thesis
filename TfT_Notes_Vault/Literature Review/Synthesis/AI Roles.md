# AI Roles

This note develops the catalog of AI roles that emerge from the literature — Socratic Tutor, Provocateur, Facilitator, Ignorant Co-Learner, Coach, and others as they appear. Each role is a stance the system adopts, instantiated through specific strategies, targeting specific cognitive functions. This note is the backbone of the conceptual framework (Role → Strategy → Cognitive Function). When a role accumulates enough detail to warrant its own treatment, it can split into a dedicated note.
